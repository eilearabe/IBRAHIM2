IBRAHIM Meral

Photographer

  • About me

    Actually, my journey towards photography started with a passion for painting. While continuing my way with my charcoal and oil paintings that I have done since my childhood and continued successfully until my high school years; my future, unfortunately, has been shaped differently due to the obstacles placed in my way. During my university years; my enthusiasm for painting turned into a passion for photography "by necessity".

  • ABOUT ME 2

    The love for producing inside me; I think that it may be the real reason for my desire to create an especially human-based composition in my photographs. Although I have a "little bit of everything" slogan, I am more interested in street photography with a focus on people and living beings. On the other hand, although I cannot give up the colors, I believe in the magic of black and white photographs. I take pictures in my free time as an amateur and I always want to continue my journey by protecting this amateur spirit.
